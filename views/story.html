Established in 1995, Cup of Joe is a cornerstone of the downtown Cedar Falls, Iowa cultural district.

As cookie-cutter coffee establishments appear on every corner, we continue to be a permanent and unique gem in the community. Two simple principles sustain us: customer-focused service and commitment to quality. These goals are why we opened our doors and are now ingrained in our long business history.

Designed with 1950s flair and a vibrant color palette, our espresso bar is truly one-of-a-kind.
